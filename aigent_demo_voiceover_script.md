## VOICEOVER SPECIFICATIONS

**Voice Profile:**
- Gender: Male (warm, authoritative)
- Age: 35-50
- Accent: British (RP or neutral)
- Pace: 140-150 words per minute (conversational, not rushed)
- Energy: Steady, confident, with subtle emotional peaks at key moments

**Delivery Notes:**
- Pause briefly (0.3-0.5 seconds) after key phrases
- Emphasize: "transparent," "safety," "family," "liquidity," "ownership"
- Soften tone slightly when mentioning farmers' struggles
- Build energy toward the final tagline

**Audio Specifications:**
- Format: MP3 or WAV
- Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- Bitrate: 192 kbps (MP3) or 16-bit (WAV)
- Normalize to -3dB for headroom
- Add subtle compression for consistency
