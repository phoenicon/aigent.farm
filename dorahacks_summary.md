### Aigent.Farm: 150-Word Project Summary

Aigent.Farm is an autonomous AI agent built to solve the UK's farming liquidity crisis. Faced with a 2026 inheritance tax shock, farmers need capital without selling their land. Existing systems are slow, opaque, and often predatory.

Our solution, built in <48 hours, is a transparent reasoning agent that performs instant field valuations, enforces a 25% collateral safety rule, and mints real-world asset tokens (FarmNotes) on-chain. It uses a multi-chain architecture (BNB, Hedera, Bitcoin) and a walletless, gas-sponsored execution pipeline, making it accessible to any farmer.
